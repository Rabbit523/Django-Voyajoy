Privacy policy

Introduction

  1. Voyajoy (“we” or “us”) take the privacy of your information very seriously. This policy explains how and for what purposes we use the information collected about you via this site (referred to below as the “Site”). Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the Site and any services we offer via the Site, you are agreeing to be bound by this policy in respect of the information collected about you via this Site.
  2. If you have any queries about the policy, please get in touch with us using our contact details and we will do our best to answer your questions.

Personal information collected

We will collect the following personal information from you

  1. Certain information required to register with the Site including first and last name, your address and date of birth together with some basic security information.
  2. Details of any bookings you make through the Site (either through us or through third parties who use the Site as a booking platform).
  3. Your e-mail address and password.
  4. Billing information such as your credit card number and expiry date.
  5. Other information about you that you voluntarily supply to use by filling out your profile page.
  6. Although it is not compulsory to give us this information, if you do not then you cannot register as a member of the Site or make a booking for accommodation with our Hosts.
